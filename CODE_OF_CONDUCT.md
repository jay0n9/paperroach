# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want PaperRoach to be a welcoming, constructive community for people with
different backgrounds, identities, research areas, and levels of experience.
Participation in project spaces means treating others with respect and good
faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and evidence rather than people.
- Be clear, patient, and generous when reviewing or asking questions.
- Respect privacy, especially around research notes, PDFs, Zotero libraries,
  and personal data.
- Accept feedback and correct mistakes when they are pointed out.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, threats, doxxing,
deliberate disclosure of private research material, and sustained disruption
of collaboration are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close or lock discussions, reject
contributions, or suspend participation when behavior violates this policy.
Reports are handled as confidentially as practical. To report a concern,
contact the repository owner privately through GitHub and avoid posting
sensitive details publicly.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
